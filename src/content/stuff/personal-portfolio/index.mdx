this is the website you're currently viewing. I built it to showcase my work and share my thoughts on life in general.

### Features

- **WebGL noise background** - animated perlin noise with dithering, reacts to music
- **Music player** - press `m` to play music, background goes brrr
- **Theme toggle** - press `0` to switch between light and dark modes
- **Keyboard navigation** - press `n` for navigation menu
- **MDX blog** - write posts in Markdown with component support

### Technical Details

- the background uses WebGL with a custom shader featuring perlin noise. it responds to audio input, warping the texture based on bass and overall levels.
- state persists across page navigations - both the background animation and music playback continue seamlessly by using [Astro ClientRouter](https://docs.astro.build/en/guides/view-transitions/#persisting-state-across-view-transitions).
