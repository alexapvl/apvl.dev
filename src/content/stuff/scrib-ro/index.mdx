[scrib.ro](https://www.scrib.ro) is a landing page I built for my father and for [ROBITE](https://www.robite.ro), the company behind the SCRIB product.

SCRIB is a HR and payroll software ecosystem for medium and large companies.

the website presents both sides of the product: SCRIB Desktop, the on-prem HR and payroll application, and SCRIB Web EDP, the employee digital portal.

I also actively maintain the website by updating product information, adding new pages and keeping the content aligned with how SCRIB evolves.

special thanks to [Cristian]("https://cretu.dev") for helping me out on this one, it was a pleasure.

### Features

- **Bilingual website** - Romanian and English pages with localized copy
- **Product-focused landing pages** - separate sections for SCRIB Desktop and SCRIB Web EDP
- **Edition pages** - presentation for Enterprise, Professional and Payroll editions
- **Interactive product sections** - animated hero, client carousel, modals, mobile sheets and visual feature blocks
- **SEO-focused structure** - metadata, sitemap, robots config and structured product/business information
- **Content pages** - blog, presentations, privacy and cookies pages

### Technical Details

- the website is built with Astro, TypeScript, React islands and Tailwind CSS.
- React is used only where interactivity is needed: animated headers, background effects, navigation primitives, mobile sheets and motion components.
- the content is localized through a small custom i18n layer with Romanian as the default language and English routes available under `/en`.
- the visual direction is much more commercial than this site: orange brand accents, large product screenshots, animated backgrounds and clear call-to-action sections.
- the project includes dedicated pages for product features, licensing, editions, offers, video presentations and the SCRIB web applications.
- ongoing work includes content updates, new pages and small improvements as ROBITE's product communication changes.
