MaxonScribe is a medical dictation app that turns Romanian voice recordings into structured, editable patient documents.

it started as a university project for a course called Innovative Product Design, then grew into client work. I built it alongside [Cristian](https://cretu.dev), [Mihai](https://mihaicristian.dev), [Vasile](https://draguta.dev), and [Alexandru](https://agportfolio-a13e2a8e20e4.herokuapp.com), and I am currently its only maintainer. I continue to maintain it pro bono, with the infrastructure designed to keep the app running without hosting costs.

the goal is to take some of the repetitive typing out of clinical documentation. A doctor can record or upload a consultation, review the Romanian transcript, extract either a compact set of findings or a complete clinical note, edit the result, and export it as a DOCX document.

### Features

- **Romanian medical transcription** - records in the browser or accepts an existing audio file, then transcribes it with Whisper
- **Two extraction modes** - produces compact key points for quick reuse or a complete structured clinical document
- **Cardiology-aware output** - understands common Romanian abbreviations, normalizes measurements and units, and removes duplicate findings
- **Patient history** - keeps recordings, transcripts, generated documents, and their processing status attached to each patient
- **Editable documents** - generated notes remain fully editable and are saved automatically
- **Flexible workflow** - recordings can be created before choosing a patient and assigned later
- **DOCX export** - turns the reviewed result into a formatted document ready for the doctor's existing workflow
- **Installable PWA** - works as an installable web app without adding a separate desktop client

### Technical Details

- the frontend is built with React, TypeScript, Vite, and TanStack Router.
- Convex handles authentication, storage, realtime data, background actions, and doctor-scoped access to patients and documents.
- Groq's Whisper large-v3 transcribes audio with Romanian selected explicitly.
- Gemini 2.5 Flash uses schema-constrained generation to turn transcripts into predictable medical data instead of free-form AI text.
- the extraction prompts are tuned for Romanian clinical language, with additional normalization for cardiology and echocardiography measurements.
- TipTap powers the document editor and debounced autosave, while the DOCX pipeline generates the final export.
- stopped recordings are preserved locally before upload, reducing the chance of losing a dictation after an accidental refresh or navigation.
